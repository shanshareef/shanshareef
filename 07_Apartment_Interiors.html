Apartment Interiors

Interior design for a dwelling occupied for a couple in Kerala, attempts to create a warm but minimal colour schemes and material choices. The usage of the spaces is optimized at the same time responding at the maximum needs of the users. The design concept involved making the apartment as single entity ,while distinguishing individual spaces through mild texture differences..
